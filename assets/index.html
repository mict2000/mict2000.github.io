About Me

I have no programming experience whatsoever. I am learning this skill in order to complement my vast work experience. I have most recently worked for Verizon, the Department of Homeland Security, and as a contractor for Genentech. I currently have a small surf movie operation using footage from drones. In just the past 18 months I have filmed in Hawaii, Costa Rica, Canary Islands, and from Pismo Beach to China Beach here in California.

My short term goal is to incorporate my love of surfing and hip hop music with programming skills in order to create unique multimedia experiences for people while securing employment within the creative technical environment of the Bay Area.
